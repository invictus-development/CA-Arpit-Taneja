About Us

Arpit Taneja & Associates specialise in the field of accounting, financial reporting, taxation, business advisory services, auditing and assurance through the hands of experienced and skilled Chartered Accountants.

We offer high potential into the strategic, operational, accounting, tax and regulatory advisory. We work on high efficiency and a deep sense of commitment that holds an extraordinary portfolio which represents backing of a rich blend of pure experience and expertise. Since our work culture is client-centric, the appreciation by our clients also signifies a full satisfaction level.

Over the years, we have developed expertise in varied services that complement and add value to the clients’ business. For achieving this mission we perceive that our commitment, diverse expertise and teamwork as the core strength of the firm. Our focus on delivering exceptional client service is backed by a partner driven approach to offer tailor-made solutions to meet unique and complex client needs and ensuring quality excellence and time efficiencies.
